Welcome to The Village Retreat, Vagad

The Village Retreat, Vagad is a village based homestay program hosted by Mr Manharsinh M. Chudasama, which allows you to weave oneself into the life of the village, farm and the environment around them.

It is approved by Gujarat Tourism as one of the pioneers of Agritourism programmes.

Air conditioned rooms with running water and antique furniture.

Have a chat on Gir cows, their history, breeding, national and world scenario.

Teach at IndiraDevi Pre-School

Experience with us the unexplored side of Bhal and Vagad!

We promise some unique and inspirational journeys at our homestays in Vagad.

By living with us, join our vision to ensure a sustainable and holistic development of these remote communities through tourism.

Homestays based tourism ensures better livelihood for these communities and empowerment of village women.

Experiences

Cycle down to the cottage located, right in the middle of cotton field's. Relax and walk down to the check dam, while we cook u a meal on the wood fire chula.

Watch international sports, Satellite TV is available.

Feed the desi hens and white pigeons.

Visit Velavadar Black Buck Park in the morning, mingle with the highest density of any antelopes in Asia. Carry sandwiches and take your time.

Supervise farm work, take a hike, we will provide a meal cooked in the middle of nowhere. Take in the sights, immerse yourself into the environment.

Play with rabbits, run in circles with them.

Do some gardening, prepare some manure or just manage the plants or do some watering.

Spend ur day cycling by canals and trails, some nice off road circuits also available.

Play badminton

Go cow grazing with the Nana bhai Bharwads of Vagad.

All meals after breakfast, are cooked on a woodfire chula.

Outdoor dining, encourages everyone to interact and share memories and anecdotes.
